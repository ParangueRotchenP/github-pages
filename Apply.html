ACADEMIC PROGRAMS & CURRICULUM

At Athena Vanguard Academy, we offer a comprehensive and dynamic curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and empower young women to become confident, independent thinkers and leaders. Our academic programs are tailored to meet the diverse interests and strengths of our students, fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a love of learning.

STEM PROGRAMS
Our robust STEM programs encourage students to explore and develop skills in fields that are shaping the future. We provide hands-on, project-based learning experiences that spark curiosity, foster innovation, and emphasize practical application.

HUMANITIES AND SOCIAL SCIENCES Literature And History: Our English and history courses are designed to develop critical thinking, communication skills, and cultural awareness. Through the analysis of diverse texts and the exploration of different time periods, cultures, and perspectives, students gain a deeper understanding of the world around them.

ARTS AND CREATIVE EXPRESSION
Visual and Performing Arts: We offer a broad range of creative arts programs, including visual arts, theater, dance, and music. Our arts curriculum nurtures self-expression, creativity, and teamwork, helping students build confidence and an appreciation for the arts.

ICT (Information and Communications Technology)
Program Digital Literacy and Skills Development: Our ICT program equips students with essential digital skills to thrive in today’s technology-driven world. Students gain hands-on experience in coding, programming, and digital design, working with the latest tools and technologies to prepare for the future.

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)
Program Business and Entrepreneurship: The ABM track provides students with a comprehensive understanding of financial literacy, accounting principles, business management, and entrepreneurship. This program prepares them for success in the business world by emphasizing both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

HOME ECONOMICS PROGRAM
Practical Life Skills: Our Home Economics program focuses on essential life skills for personal and professional success. From cooking and nutrition to personal finance and household management, students acquire valuable knowledge to become independent, resourceful individuals.

At Athena Vanguard Academy, we are committed to preparing young women for success in all aspects of life. Our academic programs are designed to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and develop leadership skills, equipping every student with the tools needed to excel in a rapidly changing world.

HOW TO APPLY?

Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Follow the steps below to apply for a place at Athena Vanguard Academy:

1. Submit an Application Form
2. Submit Required Documents Along with the application form, please submit the following documents:
- A copy of your child’s birth certificate
- Recent school report card
- Original Form 137-A with dry seal
- Certification of Good Moral Character, signed by the school principal or guidance counselor from the last school attended
3. Entrance Assessment
To ensure proper academic placement, all applicants must take an entrance assessment that measures skills in key areas such as English, Mathematics, and reasoning ability.
4. Interview with the Admissions Team
Shortlisted applicants will be invited for an interview with a member of our admissions team.
5. Notification of Admission Decision
After completing the assessment and interview, we will notify you of the admission decision. If your child is offered a place, you will receive details about enrollment, fees, and the start of the academic year.

ADMISSIONS CRITERIA
Grade Levels: We accept students from Grades 7 to College based on the academic programs we offer. Academic Excellence: A strong academic foundation is valued. While we take a holistic approach to admissions, good academic standing in the previous year is preferred. Character and Leadership: We seek students who embody our core values of integrity, resilience, and leadership potential. Our goal is to build a community of driven, passionate young women eager to contribute to their school and society. Extracurricular Interests: Students with diverse interests who are enthusiastic about participating in extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, leadership programs, and community service, are encouraged to apply.

TUITION & FEES
Athena Vanguard Academy provides a high-quality, well-rounded education while striving to make it accessible to families. Tuition fees vary based on grade level and specific programs. For detailed information, please contact our Admissions Office or visit the Fees section on our website. Scholarships: We offer scholarships for deserving students based on academic performance, leadership potential, and financial need. Inquire about scholarship opportunities during the application process.
