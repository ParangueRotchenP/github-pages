Our Campus Facilities

At Athena Vanguard Academy, we believe that the learning environment plays a vital role in a student’s growth and success. Our state-of-the-art campus is designed to inspire, engage, and support our students in all aspects of their education. With modern facilities and a focus on student well-being, we ensure that every corner of our school is optimized for creativity, collaboration, and academic excellence.

Upcoming Events


At Athena Vanguard Academy, we believe in creating a vibrant school community where students, families, and staff can come together to learn, celebrate, and grow. Below are some of the exciting events and activities happening at our school in the upcoming months. We invite you to participate and be part of our dynamic, engaging community!


Open House & School Tour

Date: March 3, 2025

Time: Begins at 8:00 am

Join us for our Open House, where prospective students and their families can tour our campus, meet faculty and staff, and learn more about what makes Athena Vanguard Academy a unique and inspiring place to learn. We’ll be showcasing our academic programs, extracurricular activities, and state-of-the-art facilities. Don’t miss the opportunity to experience our school community first-hand!

Science & Innovation Fair

Date: April 7, 2025

Time: 8:00 am to 4:00 pm

Our Science & Innovation Fair highlights the research, experiments, and technological innovations created by our students. From biology and chemistry projects to tech-driven inventions, this event is a celebration of creativity and problem-solving. Students will have the chance to present their work to peers, faculty, and visiting professionals.

College & Career Fair

Date: May 5, 2025

Time: 10:00 am to 3:00 pm

We invite high school students and their families to our College & Career Fair, where they can explore post-graduation opportunities. College representatives, industry professionals, and career mentors will be present to offer guidance, answer questions, and provide valuable insights on future pathways.
